## 2. Transparency & Compliance Statement

### 2.1 Game Logic & Fairness
This dApp is a single-player strategic application based on classic competitive rules. All game outcomes are determined by transparent, deterministic logic. The system is designed to provide a fair and consistent environment for users to engage in tactical anticipation and logical reasoning.

### 2.2 Token Usage & In-Game Credits
This application allows users to utilize SPL tokens (Solana Blockchain) to acquire in-game credits. 
- **Purpose:** Credits are strictly for in-app personalization and unlocking non-monetary digital features.
- **Value:** In-game credits have no real-world monetary value.

### 2.3 Strict No Cash-Out Policy
**IMPORTANT:** This dApp does not support any form of "cash-out" or "play-to-earn" withdrawal mechanisms. 
- In-game credits **cannot** be exchanged, refunded, or converted back into cryptocurrency or fiat currency.
- All acquisitions are final and intended solely for enhancing the individual user experience within the local environment.

### 2.4 Legal Compliance

- Operates as a single-player logic contest for personal cognitive engagement.
